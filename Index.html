2nd District Court of Appeal Clerk Appointment

In compliance with Los Angeles County Department of Public Health health and safety guidelines, the court has implemented steps to protect the staff and public while conducting court business. Please adhere to the following procedures:

  1. When visiting the court, a face mask or covering must be worn at all times in accordance with Los Angeles County Department of Public Health. This applies to attorneys, parties, court staff, vendors, and the general public. The only exceptions to this policy will be for documented medical reasons or an ADA accommodation approved by the court. Please note if an exception is granted, admittance into the court may be restricted to a specific time of the day or by scheduled appointment.

  2. The Clerk’s Office is inaccessible to the public unless a scheduled reservation has been made. This policy applies to attorneys, parties, copying services, and justice partners for record review. Due to limited capacity, reservations are for 2 hours maximum and held for 15 minutes. For reservations, please call (213) 830-7000 or select the following:
    • Record review/Copy service appointment
      • Table 1
      • Table 2
    • Justice partners’ appointment
      • Station 1
    • See a clerk – If an in-person meeting is requested or needed, please contact the Clerk’s Office to schedule an appointment at (213) 830-7000.

The Self-help center is inaccessible to the public. For more information, call (213)830-7234 or visit the website https://selfhelp.appellate.courts.ca.gov/
